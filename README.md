# 3D Printing Weekly News

A weekly news and discussion presentation for our local 3D printing meetup group. Each week we cover the latest printers, slicers, materials, community drama, and cool builds — then open the floor for discussion.

**[View the Presentations](https://MaxSikorski.github.io/3d-printing-weekly-news)** &nbsp;·&nbsp; **[Join the Meetup](https://www.meetup.com/3d-printing-club/)**

---

## What This Is

Each week, a new presentation is published covering:

- **3D Printing News** — New printers, slicer updates, and industry happenings
- **Show & Tell** — Cool projects members have built or printed
- **Quick Tips** — Tricks, mods, and fixes worth sharing
- **Community Topics** — Whatever the group is curious about

The presentations run live at our weekly meetups and are archived here for anyone who missed one.

## Want to Share Something?

Found a cool project, a useful tip, or some wild 3D printing news this week? **Submit it as a GitHub Issue** and we'll try to cover it in the next meetup.

**[Submit a Topic →](https://github.com/MaxSikorski/3d-printing-weekly-news/issues/new/choose)**

You can share:
- A news article or blog post you found interesting
- A YouTube video worth watching together
- A Reddit thread the community should see
- Something you built, printed, or are working on
- A question you want to put to the group

Just open an issue, fill in the template, and we'll take it from there.
